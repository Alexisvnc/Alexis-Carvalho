Who I Am

My name is Alexis Carvalho. I am a professional powerlifter ranked top 50 in the world in the U90 category in raw division. I have more than a decade of experience in lifting, I've coached over 100 athletes now, some became national champions with big and respectable totals in their weight class alongside some athletes who have excell at a pro/international level.

My coaching is built from real experience on the platform — not theory only. I understand what it takes to progress from beginner to high-level competitor, and my goal is to transfer that knowledge directly to the athletes I work with.
